Who are we?


G-Studio is a media club of GITAM Deemed to be University and a platform for skillful, ambitious, and creative people who showcase their talent and passion for photography, cinematography, direction, and screenwriting. We collaborate with other student clubs to provide an enriching experience. It hosts master classes with renowned film technicians and photo workshops with leading photographers. camera brands, and mentors. GStudio also allows students to explore their interests and hone their skills in a fun and engaging environment. Its range of activities is an excellent way for students to develop their creative talents.

What is CC


GStudio's Chalana Chitram aims to provide the audience with a unique and high-quality cinematic experience that combines art and technology to captivate viewers. Creators may learn from experts in the field and become familiar with cutting-edge filmmaking practices at ChalanaChitram. GStudio has been around for 10 years, a significant accomplishment and evidence of the team's commitment and diligence. The celebration this year will undoubtedly be a memorable occasion and give many more artists a platform to explore the fields of cinema and photography. GStudio has been a pioneer in advancing the art of filmmaking during the course of its ten-year existence and has assisted in the development of many talented individuals.

GStudio: Media club of GITAM, takes the honor to invite you to our very own Film Festival which is happening from 23rd Feb 2024 to 25th Feb 2024 at GITAM (deemed to be) University, Visakhapatnam.
