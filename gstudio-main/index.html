Who are we?

GStudio is a media club of Gitam University and a platform for skillful, ambitious, and creative people who showcase their talent and passion for photography, cinematography, direction, and screenwriting. We collaborate with other student clubs to provide an enriching experience. It hosts master classes with renowned film technicians and photo workshops with leading photographers. camera brands, and mentors. GStudio also allows students to explore their interests and hone their skills in a fun and engaging environment. Its range of activities is an excellent way for students to develop their creative talents.

What is CC:

GStudio's ChalanaChitram aims to provide the audience with a unique and high-quality cinematic experience that combines art and technology to captivate viewers. Creators may learn from experts in the field and become familiar with cuttingedge filmmaking practices at ChalanaChitram. GStudio has been around for 10 years, a significant accomplishment and evidence of the team's commitment and diligence. The celebration this year will undoubtedly be a memorable occasion and give many more artists a platform to explore the fields of cinema and photography. GStudio has been a pioneer in advancing the art of filmmaking during the course of its ten-year existence and has assisted in the development of many talented individuals.
